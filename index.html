Te Reo Maori to English Dictionary

Te Reo Maori is one of New Zealands three official languages. It is used in place names, the news, and various cultural items found all over New Zealand! As Te Reo Maori is compulsory to learn in schools, this dictionary was made to help students and teachers alike on the journey to raise awareness for this wonderful language.

Learn new Te Reo Maori words on our illustrated Te Reo Maori to English dictionary. Discover new pieces of traditional Maori culture and relate them back to concepts in English. Sort the dictionary in various way to find the specific words you need.
